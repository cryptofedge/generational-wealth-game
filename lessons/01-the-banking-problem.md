# Lesson 01: The Problem with Traditional Banking

## Overview
Most people give their money to a bank, earn almost no interest, and then borrow their own money back at high rates. This lesson explores why that model works against you — and what you can do instead.

## The Banking System Exposed

When you deposit money at a bank:
- The bank lends it out at 7–20%+ interest
- You earn 0.01–0.5% back
- The bank keeps the difference (the "spread")

This is called **fractional reserve banking** — and you're funding it.

## The True Cost of Financing

Every time you finance something through a third party (car loan, mortgage, credit card), you pay interest that **leaves your wealth system forever**. That money is gone — it doesn't come back.

| Purchase | Financed Cost | Cash Cost | Interest Lost |
|----------|--------------|-----------|---------------|
| $30,000 car (60mo @ 7%) | ~$35,640 | $30,000 | ~$5,640 |
| $400,000 home (30yr @ 7%) | ~$957,000 | $400,000 | ~$557,000 |

## Opportunity Cost

Beyond the interest paid, there's a second loss: **opportunity cost**. The money you spend on interest could have been compounding inside a properly structured life insurance policy — growing tax-free, every single year.

## The Paradigm Shift

The goal of the Infinite Banking Concept is to:
1. **Stop leaking wealth** to banks and finance companies
2. **Recapture** that interest by becoming your own banker
3. **Redirect** every dollar of interest back into your own system

## Action Step

Calculate how much interest you paid in the last 12 months across all debts (car, mortgage, credit cards, student loans). Write that number down — that's the wealth leak you're going to stop.
